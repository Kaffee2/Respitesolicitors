Our Practices Areas

Corporate Law

Corporate Legal Compliance, contracts, business formation, and governance. Corporate Law involves legal matters related to business formation, compliance, contracts, and governance. It ensures companies operate lawfully, manage risks, and follow proper governance practices.

Family Law

Divorce, child custody, adoption, and family mediation **Family Law** covers legal issues related to family relationships, including divorce, child custody, adoption, and family mediation. It helps resolve disputes and protect the rights of family members.

Real Estate Law

Property transactions and management, zoning, and land use • Real Estate Law deals with buying, selling, leasing properties, and how land is used or divided, including rules about zoning and land development. It guides how property transactions happen smoothly and legally.

Intellectual Property Law

Patents, trademarks, copyrights, and licensing • Intellectual Property Law protects creations of the mind—like inventions, brand names, logos, or creative works—through patents, trademarks, and copyrights. It also covers licensing, which allows others to use these creations legally.

Litigation

Civil litigation, dispute resolution, and trial advocacy • Litigation involves resolving legal disputes through the courts, whether it's someone suing another person or company, or settling disagreements through alternative methods like mediation, often preparing for trial if needed.

Research/Legal Opinion

Research/Legal Opinion involves a comprehensive analysis of specific legal questions, issues, or situations. It typically begins with identifying the relevant facts and understanding the context. The process then includes researching applicable laws, statutes, regulations, judicial decisions, and legal principles that pertain to the issue at hand.

Legal Advisory

Legal Advisory is the practice of providing expert legal guidance tailored to the specific needs of clients, whether individuals, corporations, or organizations. It involves analyzing complex legal issues, interpreting applicable laws, regulations, and policies, and offering strategic advice to help clients make sound decisions.

Estate Planning/Family Legacy

Estate Planning/Family Legacy is a comprehensive process that helps individuals arrange their assets and personal affairs in a way that ensures their wishes are carried out after they pass away. This typically involves creating legal documents such as wills, trusts, powers of attorney, and healthcare directives.
